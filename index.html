Naya Rivera

1987-2020

Naya Rivera in 2019

Naya Rivera was an American actress, singer, and model. She was best known for her role as Santana Lopez on the television series "Glee". Naya was born on January 12, 1987, in Santa Clarita, California. She began her acting career at a young age and quickly rose to fame with her role on "Glee". Naya was also known for her advocacy work and her dedication to various charitable causes. She tragically passed away on July 8, 2020, at the age of 33. Naya Rivera will always be remembered for her talent, kindness, and the impact she had on her fans and the entertainment industry.
